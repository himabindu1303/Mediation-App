More About Breathing Exercises

Here are some breathing exercises you can try:

Diaphragmatic Breathing (Belly Breathing)

  • Inhaling slowly through your nose while paying attention to your belly rising
  • Exhaling through your mouth for at least two to three times as long as your inhale
  • Relaxing your neck and shoulders

Deep breathing

  • Inhaling slowly through your nose to a count of four or five
  • Exhaling slowly through your nose to a count of four or five
  • Making sure your stomach rises when you inhale, not your chest
  • Visualizing pushing your belly button to your spine when you exhale

Equal breathing

  • Inhaling and exhaling through your nose for the same length of time
  • Counting during each inhale and exhale to make sure they are even
  • Repeating a word or short phrase during each inhale and exhale
  • Pausing briefly after each inhale and exhale
